Frequently Asked Questions

I accidentally deleted a photo. Can I get it back?

Yes, in most cases! MediaCleaner deletes files by moving them to your iOS "Recently Deleted" album. Open your Photos app, go to Albums -> Recently Deleted, and you can recover them there. They typically stay there for 30 days.

How does Smart Cleaning work?

Smart Cleaning scans your library for duplicates, blurry photos, screenshots, and large video files. It runs entirely on your device and suggests items for deletion, but you always have the final say before anything is removed.

I bought Premium but it's not showing up.

Please tap the "Restore Purchases" button on the upgrade screen. Ensure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for purchase.

Why does the app need Photo Library permission?

MediaCleaner needs "Full Access" to your Photo Library to analyze your storage usage and perform the deletion actions you request. Without this permission, the app cannot function.
