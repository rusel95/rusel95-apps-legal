Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2026

MediaCleaner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Photo Library Access

MediaCleaner is designed to help you organize and clean your photo gallery. To function, the app requires access to your device's Photo Library.

  • Local Processing: All analysis of your photos and videos (finding duplicates, large files, screenshots) happens locally on your device.
  • No Uploads: We do not upload your photos or videos to any external server. Your media never leaves your device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identifiers (name, age, phone number).
  • We do not collect location data, although your photos may contain location metadata which is processed locally.
  • We do not track your browsing history.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use standard Apple services and basic analytics to improve app stability:

  • Apple iCloud: If your photos are stored in iCloud, the app interacts with iOS system APIs to manage them.
  • RevenueCat: Used to process In-App Purchases (Premium features). They may collect purchase history linked to an anonymous user ID.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to delete. You can revoke the app's access to your Photo Library at any time in your device Settings.
