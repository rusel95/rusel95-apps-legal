By using Zorya, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name (or nickname) for your profile

  • Time of birth
  • Place of birth (city, country, coordinates)

This information is necessary to calculate your natal chart and generate personalized astrological reports.

User Profiles

  • You may create multiple profiles (e.g., for family members or friends)
  • Profile data includes names and birth details as listed above

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Language and locale settings

Usage Analytics (Anonymous)

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Session duration

Purchase Information

  • Record of in-app purchases (processed by Apple)
  • Credit balance within the app

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses (unless you contact support)
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise real-time location
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or camera access
  • Social media accounts
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device. This includes birth details and profiles, generated reports, and app preferences.

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • App sandbox isolation
  • Your device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID

3.2 Cloud Processing

When generating reports, the following data is sent to our secure servers:

  • Birth date, time, and coordinates (no names)
  • Selected report area

This data is:

  • Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Processed in real-time
  • Not stored on our servers after processing
  • Not linked to any personal identifiers
