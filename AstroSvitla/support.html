Getting Started

How do I create my natal chart?

  1. Open Zorya and tap "Create Profile"
  2. Enter your name, birth date, birth time, and birth location
  3. Your natal chart will be calculated automatically
  4. Select any life area to generate a detailed report

Why is my birth time important?

Your exact birth time determines the position of the Ascendant (rising sign) and house placements in your chart. These are crucial for accurate astrological readings. Even a 15-minute difference can significantly affect your chart.

If you don't know your exact birth time:
  • Check your birth certificate (many countries include time)
  • Ask family members who were present
  • Request hospital records
  • As a last resort, use 12:00 PM noon (results will be less personalized)

What life areas can I explore?

Zorya offers insights into multiple life areas:

  • Love & Relationships - Romance, compatibility, emotional patterns
  • Career & Purpose - Professional path, talents, success indicators
  • Health & Wellness - Physical constitution, vitality, self-care
  • Finances & Abundance - Money patterns, prosperity potential
  • Personal Growth - Life lessons, spiritual development
  • Family & Home - Roots, domestic life, parenting

Purchases & Credits

How do credits work?

Each detailed report requires one credit. When you purchase credits:

  • Credits are added to your account instantly
  • One credit = one personalized report for any life area
  • Credits never expire
  • Credits are tied to your Apple ID

How do I restore my purchases?

If your credits aren't showing after reinstalling the app or switching devices:

  1. Open Zorya
  2. Go to any report purchase screen
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"
  4. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for original purchase
  5. Your credits will be restored

I was charged but didn't receive credits

This can happen due to network issues. Please try:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Tap "Restore Purchases"
  3. Wait a few minutes and check again

If credits still don't appear after 24 hours, contact us with:

  • Your Apple ID email
  • Approximate purchase date and time
  • Transaction receipt (from Apple email)

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple, not by Zorya directly. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the Zorya purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund"

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or freezing

  1. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  2. Restart the app completely (swipe up to close, then reopen)
  3. Restart your device
  4. If issues persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app

Note: Reinstalling won't lose your purchases - just use "Restore Purchases"

Reports are taking too long to generate

Report generation typically takes 30-60 seconds. If it's taking longer:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try again in a few minutes (servers may be busy)
  • Make sure you're not on a VPN that might slow the connection

Location search isn't finding my city

Try these tips:

  • Use the English name of the city
  • Include the country name (e.g., "Kyiv, Ukraine")
  • Try a nearby larger city if your town is very small
  • Use coordinates if available

My chart looks different from other apps

Different astrology systems use different house systems and calculation methods. Zorya uses the Placidus house system, which is the most popular in Western astrology. Minor differences in planetary positions can occur due to different ephemeris sources.
